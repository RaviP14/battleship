  1. Firstly, you will need to place your ships onto the board:
    • To do this click onto a ship button and hover over the board - using the cursor.
    • To set the ship position, simply click where you want the ship to be placed.
    • To switch ship orientation simply click on th Vertical/Horizontal button.
    • Switching orientation changes every ships orientation - you will need to click back onto the ship button you want to place.
  2. Once a ship is palced its button will be greyed out.
  3. Click the Play Game button once all the ships have been placed.
  4. A Second board should appear, you can attack by clicking any position on the second board.
